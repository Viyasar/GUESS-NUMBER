WELCOME TO THE GAME

  • The game will start by generating a random number between 1 and 10, and your goal is to guess the number correctly.
  • If your guess is correct, you win the game!
  • If your guess is incorrect, the game will provide you with a hint to help you guess the correct number.
  • The hint will tell you if your guess is too high or too low.
  • You will have a total of 3 attempts to guess the number correctly.
  • If you fail to guess the number within 3 attempts, the game will end and you will lose.
  • Are you ready to play? CLICK START BUTTON!
