WELCOME TO THE GAME

  1. The game will start by generating a random number between 1 and 10, and your goal is to guess the number correctly.
  2. If your guess is correct, you win the game!
  3. If your guess is incorrect, the game will provide you with a hint to help you guess the correct number.
  4. The hint will tell you if your guess is too high or too low.
  5. You will have a total of 3 attempts to guess the number correctly.
  6. If you fail to guess the number within 3 attempts, the game will end and you will lose.
  7. Are you ready to play? CLICK START BUTTON!
